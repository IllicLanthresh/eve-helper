EVE Sell Helper

Paste your inventory, pick a trade hub, and pull live prices straight from ESI (no login). Every item is valued against the actual buy-order book — depth, min-volume scams and all — and ranked by expected net after fees, with the best plan per item: instant sale, sell order, or a split.

1 · Input & prices

Net/unit — instant: price × (1 − tax) against real book depth · listed: L × (1 − tax − broker). Importing at L auto-fills any buy orders priced ≥ L first (at their price), so SPLIT plans execute themselves.

Notes & assumptions
  • Instant valuation walks the real buy book top-down: order depth (volume_remain) and min_volume (margin-scam bait) are respected, so one 1-unit buy order at a silly price no longer values your whole stack.
  • The ORDER/SPLIT plan models what actually happens when you import at L: buy orders priced ≥ L fill immediately at their price (no broker fee, tax only), the rest is listed at L (broker fee + tax). SPLIT just means that first part is non-empty.
  • Order fills are not guaranteed — an order at a history-derived price may take days or never fill. Relist fees from repricing are not modelled.
  • Price history is per region (ESI has no station-level history) using each day's average price; the day range and statistic are applied locally, so changing them needs no refetch.
  • suspect price = top buy above best sell (thin/odd market) · ≫/≪ history = current sell far from the history statistic · depth x/y = the buy book can only absorb x of your y units.
  • unsellable? = Ice Storm / Expired filaments the market won't accept; auto-excluded from the export (you can re-tick them).
  • All ESI endpoints used are public — no login, nothing leaves your machine except the price queries.
  • Prices respect EVE's 4-significant-digit rule (undercut = one tick of the price's own band).

Format: Item name ⇥ Price — prices are ISK per unit; the game sells the whole stack. The list contains the ticked rows passing the filters above. An import price only matters for ORDER and SPLIT items (priced at the list price L; buy orders above L fill instantly on import at their own price, the rest lists at L) — so the selection buttons pick only those. INSTANT items have no price to choose: select them in the hangar and sell directly. You can still tick one manually to import it at its stack-clearing floor price.
