Notes & assumptions
  • Broker fee is charged up front on the full order value when you list a sell order; it is not charged when your sale fills an existing buy order (instant sale). Sales tax applies to every sale.
  • Relist fees from repricing later are not modelled — if you expect to have to undercut repeatedly, instant selling gets relatively better.
  • suspect price = buy > sell. These are exactly the items Janice marks as “missing or low quality price information” (thin markets) — double-check them in game before trusting the number.
  • unsellable? = Ice Storm / Expired filaments etc. that the market won't accept; they are auto-excluded from the export (you can re-tick them).
  • ESI prices are the live best buy / best sell at Jita IV-4 (buy orders that cover the station are included). Public endpoints only — no ESI login required.
  • Quantities come from the inventory paste when both are given; mismatches are flagged.
  • EVE only accepts prices with 4 significant digits; the one-tick undercut respects that.
