4 · Notes & assumptions

  • Yields are gross, basic-quality ore output per 1,000 m³ of chunk. Improved variants (e.g. Brimful, Glistening) give +15%, jackpot variants ×2.
  • Multiply by your net refine rate for sellable units — the ranking itself is unaffected, since refining scales every ore the same way.
  • Moon rates assume 30,000 m³ of chunk per hour of drill prep (Athanor/Tatara). A Metenox Moon Drill is slower and auto-refines at a fixed efficiency.
  • R8 and better materials only occur on lowsec/nullsec moons; highsec and wormhole moons cap at R4 ubiquitous ores.
  • Live prices are best Jita 4-4 station orders from public ESI (The Forge region) — no login, nothing leaves your machine except the price queries.
  • Ore/yield data: EVE University Wiki — Moon mining → Moon Ore Refining.
