Sites are the 7 signature Sov-Hub Ore Prospecting Array deposits (tier-2, 4 h 20 m respawn); small sites are tier-1 and large ones take 10 h, but their compositions scale proportionally, so the ranking holds. Totals are gross — multiply by your net refine rate. Data: EVE University Wiki.
